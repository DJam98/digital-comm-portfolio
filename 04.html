Rationale

This stakeholder map is a way to visually depict the stakeholders impacted by the recent data breaches on the Twitter / X platform. This map has two axes—those who have direct power over the platform and can control things like security. Then, the other axis displays how much influence these groups might have over the platform or at least impact the potential changes that Twitter / X could make towards remedying the issues that have occurred.
